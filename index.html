What are we all about?

Watch it outside Boston edition is an annual film festival, free for Bostonian viewers of all ages, and for any type of weather. The goal is to share local directors as well as some international treasures. We have carefully selected a series of 9 incredible films for you. All you need is proof of residency and maybe some popcorn!
